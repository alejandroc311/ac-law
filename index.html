FAQs

  • Will I lose my home?
    A bankruptcy filing can halt a scheduled sale. Many homeowners keep their homes by curing arrears in a Chapter 13 plan. Learn more in: Chapter 13 .
  • Will I lose my stuff?
    Most filers keep their essential property using exemptions (home equity, vehicle, household goods, wages, etc.). See what’s protected in: Bankruptcy exemptions .
  • How fast will it stop collections?
    Immediately upon filing, the automatic stay stops calls, lawsuits, garnishments, and foreclosures. See how it works: Automatic Stay .
  • Can I include my student loans?
    Generally, student loans are not discharged unless you prove undue hardship through a separate process. Get the limits here: What bankruptcy can’t do .
  • Do I have to go to court?
    You’ll attend a short 341 “meeting of creditors” (usually by phone or video). It’s not a courtroom hearing. What to expect: Your 341 meeting .
